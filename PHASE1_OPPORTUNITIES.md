# PHASE 1: TOP 5 OPPORTUNITIES

## Evaluation Framework
Each opportunity rated 1-10 on:
- Speed to first cash
- Ease of sale
- Pain intensity
- Repeatability
- Low time burden

---

| Criteria | Score | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Speed to cash | 9/10 | Providers fear audit failure - will pay FAST |
| Ease of sale | 7/10 | Can find via NDIS provider directories |
| Pain intensity | 10/10 | Mandatory compliance = lose registration if fail |
